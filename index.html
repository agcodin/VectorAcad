FAQ

Common questions

Are sessions online or in person?
All sessions are live online on an interactive shared whiteboard, so students can work problems in real time with Dinesh. It works just like sitting side-by-side — without the commute.
Who is the tutor?
Every session is taught by Dinesh, VectorAcad's founder and sole tutor. He has nearly 15 years of physics teaching experience across every level — AP, Honors, and Regular — so students keep the same teacher from the first session through exam day.
How often should we meet?
Most students meet once a week during the school year and step up to twice a week in the 6–8 weeks before the AP exam. Dinesh will recommend a cadence after the initial conversation.
Can you help if the AP exam is only a few weeks away?
Yes — Dinesh runs focused exam sprints that triage the highest-value topics for your target score, drill past free-response questions, and sharpen test strategy.
What does it cost?
Pricing depends on course level and session frequency. Reach out using the contact details below and Dinesh will walk you through the options — with no pressure to continue.
